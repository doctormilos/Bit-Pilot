Q: What is Bit-Pilot?

Bit-Pilot is free service that provides links to latest Airdrops.

Q: What is an Airdrop?

Basically Airdrop is free cryptocurrency aka coins/tokens. These coins are distributed by ICOs, for more info read other FAQs.

Q: Why ICOs give away free tokens?

Tokens are usually distributed as a marketing campaign before the sale of other tokens.

Q: Is it really valuable? Free money??

Yes! It doesn't cost anything to participate in an airdrop but you get tokens that you can sell later and get money. Go check out our How to tutorial and start earning right now!

Q: Where can I sell my tokens?

Check ICO site of the token you want to sell for news about listing.

Q: Where can I store my tokens?

For storing tokens you need Ethereum Wallet Address that is compatible with ERC-20. You can't use a wallet address from an exchange because you won't get any tokens. So how you get one? We recommend MyEtherWallet and hardware wallets like the Trezor and Ledger Nano S.

Q: What does the star rating of an airdrop mean?

The star rating is our personal rating of project and it's airdrop value.

Q: I didn't receive tokens after completing airdrop, why?

Tokens are usually distributed after the token sale so it's totally normal. Sometimes, before coins get delivered to your wallet it can take up to several months. Also sometimes but rarely project fails and no tokens are distributed.
